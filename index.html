What is it

THE LIGHTS

The FlashCourt Reaction Light System is a set of 6 wireless, hexagonal training lights that flash colors, sequences, and patterns — forcing athletes to see, process, decide, and move faster than their competition.

Unlike fitness or strength tools, reaction lights train the milliseconds between perception and action — the gap where games are won and lost.

Go / No-Go

One color is GO. The rest are NO-GO. Lights flash at random. Tap only on your color. Trains inhibitory control — the ability to NOT move when you shouldn't.
